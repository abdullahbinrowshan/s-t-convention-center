Our refund and cancellation policy for events and bookings at our convention center is as follows: If you need to cancel your event or booking more than 30 days before the scheduled date, you will receive a full refund. If you cancel your event or booking between 15 and 30 days before the scheduled date, you will receive a 50% refund. If you cancel your event or booking less than 15 days before the scheduled date, no refund will be issued. In the event of inclement weather or other circumstances outside of our control, the convention center reserves the right to reschedule events and bookings as necessary. In such cases, a full refund will not be issued, but we will work with you to find a mutually agreeable solution.

Here are some strategies for staying within budget: Clearly define budget parameters and allocate funds for specific expenses. Establish a system for tracking expenses, such as using spreadsheets or accounting software. Regularly review and compare actual expenses against the budget to identify and address any discrepancies. Prioritize and adjust spending as needed to ensure the most important expenses are covered. Regularly communicate with all stakeholders to ensure everyone is aware of the budget status and working towards the same financial goals.
